Look at the image on the right and follow these directions.

Hints:

  • You can use the Zoom In button to look more closely.
  • You can move the image around by "dragging" the image around. That is, by holding the mouse button down while you move it.
  • You can reset the zooming and dragging by clicking the Reset button.

Does the image look complete top to bottom? If so, press the """Other Side" button.

Does the other siee also look complete top to bottom?

If either side of the ballot did not look complete, press the Backup One button. The image from the prior ballot will appear on the right side.

Repeat checking the front and back of the prior ballot. Almost always the previous ballot will be fine, but if not press "Backup One" again.

Once you have found the images of the last good ballot on the screen, notice its imprint on the left side of the front.

It is possible that one or two of the ballots in the output bin of the scanner have imprint numbers higher that the Last Good Number, which is being displayed. If so: move those ballots back to input bin of the scanner, face down top edge leading.

Once you have things arranged so that the number in the Next Ballot field is matched to the top ballot in the input tray, press Resume.
