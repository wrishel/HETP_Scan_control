Look at the image on the right and follow these directions.

Hints:
  • You can use the Zoom In button to look more closely.
  • You can move the image around by "dragging". That is, by holding the mouse button down while you move it.
  • You can reset the zooming and dragging by clicking the Reset button.
Does the image look complete top to bottom? If not, press "Not OK".

Look for the imprint on the left side of the ballot, somewhere in the middle. It should be the same number that is in the Last Good Ballot Number.

If it is OK, and this is a two-sided ballot, press the Other Side button to examine the back. Does it looks OK, press the Resume button to continue scanning. If not, press Not OK.
